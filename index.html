About UPYOG

Uttarakhand Urban Development Department

🏔️ About the Platform

UPYOG (Urban Portal for Yojana and Online Governance) is the state-wide urban governance platform of the Uttarakhand Urban Development Department (UDD), designed to digitize and streamline municipal services across all 108 Urban Local Bodies of Uttarakhand.

The platform brings together 12 core modules covering Revenue & Finance, Infrastructure & Utilities, Public Health & Welfare, and Administration & Planning — accessible through a citizen web portal, mobile app, and ULB employee dashboards.

🎯 Key Objectives

1
Digitize Municipal Services
End-to-end online services for all civic functions across 108 ULBs
2
Increase Revenue Collection
GIS-based property mapping and online payment integration
3
Improve Grievance Redressal
SLA-bound grievance system with real-time tracking
4
State-wide Data Hub
Centralized UDD data for policy and planning decisions
